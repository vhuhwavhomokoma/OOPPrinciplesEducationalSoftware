Offline Activity: Understanding the Basics
Activity: Encapsulation in Everyday Objects.
  • Task:

    In small groups, think of a real-world object (e.g., a water bottle, car, or phone).

  • Discussion:
    • What attributes (data) does it have? (e.g., for a water bottle: colour, capacity).
    • What actions (methods) can be done with it? (e.g., for a water bottle: fill, drink).
    • What should be private (data) and what should be public (methods)?
