Offline Activity
Polymorphism Role-Play
Activity: Ask students to role-play a scenario where one person takes on multiple roles. For example, one student plays a "teacher," "coach," and "parent," showing how the same person behaves differently in each role. Other students can discuss how this relates to polymorphism in programming.
Time Frame: 20–25 minutes.
Objective: Visually and physically demonstrate the concept of one entity (person) taking multiple forms (roles).
