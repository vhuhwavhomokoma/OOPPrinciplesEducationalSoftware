Application


Let's say you have an Animal class that has attributes like species, colour and weight and behaviour such as eat. Animal acts as a parent class. Species, colour and weight are common attributes that all animals have and eat is a behaviour all animals have. A Dog is a type of Animal. A Dog class can inherit these attributes from the Animal class but might add specific attributes such as breed or specific behaviour such as bark. A Lion is also a type of Animal and has specific behaviour such as roar.
