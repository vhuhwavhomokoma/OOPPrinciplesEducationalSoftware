Offline Games

Preconditions:

  • This activity should be done with a minimum of 4 participants
  • The participants should be in person.
  • The activity should be done in a classroom or outside.
  • A pen and paper

Activity

Divide the number of students into 2 or more groups. Each group should have a minimum of 2 participants.

Each group should look around the area and identify all the objects. List the attributes and methods of each object identified on a piece of paper. The students should then draw UML Class diagrams of the objects.

After 15 minutes, each group should present their UML Diagrams, emphasizing the class name, attributes, methods, private and public members.
